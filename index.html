SHENG Xihua 盛锡华

♦ Dr. Xihua Sheng is currently a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where he is advised by Prof. Changwen Chen.
♦ Previously, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong from 2024/07-2026/02, where he was advised by Prof. Shiqi Wang.
♦ Dr. Sheng received the Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), Hefei, Anhui, China, in 2024/06. where he was advised by Prof. Li Li, Prof. Dong Liu, and Prof. Zhiwei Xiong.
♦ Before that, he received the B.S. degree in Automation from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China, in 2019/06.
